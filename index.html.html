Services

All services are provided one-on-one in your home and tailored to your goals and environment.

Orthopedic Physiotherapy

Assessment and treatment for joint, muscle, and soft-tissue injuries including back pain, neck pain, shoulder injuries, and knee pain.

Post-Surgical Rehabilitation

Rehabilitation following procedures such as joint replacements, ligament repairs, and fracture recovery.

Sports Injury Rehabilitation

Treatment for acute and overuse injuries to support safe return to sport and activity.

Chronic Pain Management

Evidence-based treatment focusing on long-term pain relief, movement confidence, and self-management.

Mobility & Balance Training

Programs designed to improve strength, balance, and confidence with daily activities and reduce fall risk.

Postural & Ergonomic Education

Guidance for posture, workstation setup, and movement habits to prevent injury and manage pain.

Cupping Therapy

Therapeutic cupping to promote blood flow, reduce muscle tension, and relieve pain.

Acupuncture

Targeted acupuncture to assist in pain management and support the body’s natural healing process.

IASTM - Instrument Assisted Soft Tissue Mobilization

Specialized tool-assisted technique to break down scar tissue, improve mobility, and reduce pain.

Mulligan's Approach - Mobilization with Movement

Manual therapy technique combining joint mobilization with active patient movement to restore function and reduce pain.

Active Release Therapy

Hands-on therapy to release tight muscles, tendons, and fascia to improve movement and reduce discomfort.

Oncology (Cancer) Rehabilitation

Specialized physiotherapy for cancer patients to improve strength, mobility, and quality of life during and after treatment.
